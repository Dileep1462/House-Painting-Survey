Respondent Instructions/Notes

Welcome!

Thank you for participating in our survey on house paints. Your feedback is important and will help us better understand consumer preferences and improve paint products.

Please read the following instructions carefully before you begin:

1. Be Honest
There are no right or wrong answers. Please answer all questions truthfully based on your own experience and opinions.
2. Stay Focused:
The survey should take about 5-10 minutes to complete. Try to complete it in one sitting without interruptions.
3. Eligibility:
This survey is intended for individuals who have been involved in choosing or purchasing house paints within the last 2 years.
4. Privacy:
All your responses will be kept confidential and used solely for research purposes.
5. Device Recommendation:
For the best experience, we recommend completing the survey on a desktop or laptop.
By clicking “Start” ,you acknowledge that you have read and understood the instructions.
